Why it matters

Value to the community

  • Reach everyone, everywhere — phones, screens, web, WhatsApp.
  • Always up to date — change once; every surface syncs.
  • Trusted, official source — no scattered groups or copy-paste errors.
  • Save running costs — MasjidBox replaced, no per-screen fees.
  • Janāzah & urgent notices — push, WhatsApp, screens — in minutes.
  • Mosque owns it all — no vendor lock-in, open architecture.
Designed for a small volunteer team to run with confidence.
